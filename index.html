Reading the cards

W win D draw L loss Through

Each person's six teams sit three across, two rows. Under each is its win / draw / loss record across the three group games, with the team's points top-right. Once a team makes the knockouts, a gold stage badge (QG, QF, SF, FN, WC) appears next to its name showing how far it has gone. Knockout bonuses stack as a team goes deeper; reaching the round of 16 scores nothing on its own.

How this updates

This page reads results.json, which a scheduled job rewrites once a day with the latest scores — so normally you do nothing. If you ever need to correct something by hand, edit results.json: each team has a g list of group results ("W", "D", "L") and an optional reached stage ("R32" qualified, then "QF", "SF", "F", "W") once the knockouts begin. Save and re-publish; the table recalculates and re-sorts on its own.
